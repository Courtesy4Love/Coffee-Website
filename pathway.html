Collection Schedule and Routes

The Barangay Solid Waste Management team follows specific routes and schedules to ensure efficient and consistent garbage collection. Please check the schedule for your area to know when to expect collection services.

Important Reminders

  • Please place garbage bins or bags outside your home by 6:00 AM on your scheduled collection day.
  • Make sure waste is properly segregated as per the guidelines (biodegradable, non-biodegradable, and recyclable).
  • Use designated garbage bags or containers to prevent spillage and scattering by animals.
  • Report missed collections within 24 hours using our reporting system.

Garbage Collection System Efficiency

Our Barangay implements several measures to ensure efficient waste collection:

  • Route Optimization: Collection routes are designed to minimize distance and maximize efficiency.
  • Time Management: Specific collection times help prevent traffic congestion and ensure complete coverage.
  • Data Tracking: The QR code system allows for real-time monitoring of collection progress.
  • Feedback Loop: Missed collection reports help improve system performance.

Preparation Guidelines for Collection

To ensure smooth and efficient waste collection, please follow these guidelines when preparing your waste:

Biodegradable Waste

  • Use green biodegradable bags or containers with proper labeling
  • Drain excess liquids from food waste
  • Keep meat/fish scraps secured to prevent odor and pests
  • Consider home composting for yard waste and vegetable scraps

Recyclable Materials

  • Clean containers before disposal (rinse bottles, jars, cans)
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space
  • Remove caps from plastic bottles (they can be recycled separately)
  • Bundle newspapers and magazines together

Non-Recyclable Waste

  • Use standard black garbage bags, securely tied
  • Double-bag items that might leak or break
  • Keep bags at manageable weight (under 10kg)
  • Wrap sharp objects in paper and label them
